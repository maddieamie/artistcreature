Artist Creature Personality Quiz

Are you new to drawing? Interested in what kind of artist you may be? Take our quiz below to see what kind of artist creature you are!

Artist Creature Quiz

Question 1: What getaway vacation appeals to you the most?

Question 2: What do you consider to be your defining trait?

Question 3: What is your favorite kind of food?

Question 4: What genre of art appeals to you the most?

Question 5: What's a favorite thing you enjoy doing?

Question 6: Which "adult beverage" is your preferred? [if you're underage, just pretend you know what we are talking about... or go with what sounds pretty :)]

Question 7: What would you do if you could stop time?

Question 8: What hobbies do you enjoy, or would like to try, outside of art?

Question 9: What is your favorite genre of media?

Question 10: What's your Dungeons and Dragons or RPG class?

Artist Creature: Dog

A favorite of humankind since we began writing it all down, dogs are the type of artist that taps into what makes really human. You work with joyful energy, and your art is filled with the pleasures in life. Though you may get easily distracted by companionship or other things (butterflies, maybe), you work hard when it’s time to buckle down. You have a weakness for large markers and big brushstrokes. Embrace how well you work with others and let it influence your practice. We never accomplish anything alone, and you’re the one people want around. Keep it up!

Artist Creature: Cat

In the time of ancient Egypt, cats were worshipped by many and did what they pleased the rest of the time. Curious, cautious, and adorable, you approach each art piece from a new angle than where you came from before. Though some may see you as a little haughty, you have a deep affection for those close to you and bring beauty to those in your circle. Mysterious and coy, your wit is the life of the party. You have a weakness for bold, black linework. Embrace the inner workings of your soul and let them onto your canvas. The world will be more accepting than you think. Onward!

Artist Creature: Wolf

The relationships of the wolf and their packs have saturated human society with analogies and lurid fan-fictions. Though you are seen as being on your own plane, above it all, you are your best self when surrounded by friends and family. Stunning, powerful, and resilient, you blaze through obstacles with a dogged dedication. You art is often created in great bursts of passion. You have a weakness for careful brushstrokes and precision techniques. Let it go every once in a while and be surprised by how far you’ve come on your creative journey. Though you may not know it, others look up to your process, so share the love!

Artist Creature: Lion

Known as the king of the animal kingdom, the lion shows its majesty in the limelight. Your strength of mind, body, and soul gives courage to those around you. You either lead the pack or draw others towards you with your light. Your art is always made with great conviction that you have something amazing to share. Though some may see you as a little full of it, you tend to win them over with persistence. You have a pulse on what’s popular, and are seen as someone “in the know.” As an artist, you favor references to pop culture and primary colors. You’ve got the grit, keep on keeping on!

Artist Creature: Sea Turtle

Known as one of nature’s greatest treasures, the sea turtle shows its beauty and delicacy in all things. Patient and kind, you see your relationship with your art as something to be nurtured and treasured. You capture the beauty of nature in your art, as well as life’s greatest joys. Though some may see your pace in art as too slow, you know that life is all about the little moments in great places. Stay true to what makes you happy in your art, and you will keep bringing happiness to others. Your greatest weapon is your self-positivity.

Artist Creature: Fox

The fox’s mystic beauty and cunning nature have fascinated humans since olden times. Mischievous and clever, the fox loves artistic themes that dazzle and mystify the viewer. Some may see you as being too tricksy, but it may just be that your brand of morality is more open to more perspectives than the general population's. As an artist, you tend towards pieces with great movement and scale. You are entranced with bewitching viwers and other artists alike. Stay true to your magic, and good things will bloom in you art practice. Keep to your strength of will.

Artist Creature: Horse

Horses have thanklessly assisted humans since we figured anything out. Steady and determined, the horse artist creature is one who knows their own mind and their own goals. They work tirelessly with their visions as an artist, even if others don't understand what they are working towards. As an artist, you favor abstract ideas and large-scale concepts in your work. Your greatest strength is in your work ethic and your ability to keep moving through all the BS. Keep true to your beautimous self and people will worship your artistic feats.

Artist Creature: Penguin

Adorable but with hidden grace, the penguin shines in turbulent times. Penguins are loyal creatures whose loved ones feel cherished by their presence. As an artist, penguins tend to either follow the rules of technique strictly or bloom into artistic chefs of the art world. They enjoy it best when their work brings joy and good feelings to others. You are on the right path if you just keep following your exquisite taste and stay true to your principles. Your work is a wonderful place for others to rest. Keep your spirit warm.

Artist Creature: Panda

Fuzzy, cute, and exacting in their taste, pandas are beloved by all. As artists, they are obsessed with what is cute, comfy, and in their wheelhouse. As such, they become experts in what makes others smile. Their technical skills tend towards the clean, round, and bright of the art world, and they usually go in as illustrators. Your ability to make art that is joyous is your greatest strength. Keep looking for what makes you happy, as it will spread out to everyone around you. Everyone thinks you're too cute!

Artist Creature: Pink Axolotl

A rare little bean, the pink axolotl is a unique creature with a demeanor that people find either enchanting or bizarre. As artists, they push the envelope forward in their chosen little pond. They do things that no one has thought of before or spin ideas that make others look at things from a different perspective. They are tied deeply to their roots, whether it be family, heritage, or a place. They enjoy the leisurely life, but only when they have worked hard to earn it. They know what it means to make it in this world: it takes grit. You've got the grit. Don't let anyone let you feel like you don't belong. Make your own splash.
